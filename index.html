Sound engineering, music production and composing services in cutting-edge studio in the heart of Budapest

After several of years of recording and producing music for various projects across three continents, our team assembled in downtown Budapest to establish our own recording studio. Our main goal is to provide a space for up and coming artists and bands where they can record their songs and get down to the nitty-gritty of mixing and mastering. With access to state-of-the-art technology as well as our expert sound engineering and producing crew, our mission is to help you along the way to finding and creating that unique sound. In addition to classic studio work, we are also providing songwriting services for professional purposes.
