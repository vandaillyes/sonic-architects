Janos Kovacs | Chief sound engineer

Janos is a versatile sound engineer with a passion for vintage blues guitars. Janos learned his profession in Hungary's Palace of Arts, a musical institution with a world class studio. He is well known for his ruthless perfectionism, enormous amounts of positive energy and his always up-to-date knowledge of the latest trends. Janos is also a lecturer at the prestigious Liszt Academy where he is teaching a course in Music Production, Technology, and Innovation.

Eddie Dalston | Producer & sound engineer

Eddie is our talented producer and sound engineer with a truly international background. Eddie started out as an apprentice at Electric Lady Studios in New York's Greenwich Village where he has had exposure to a number of genres and had the chance to learn the tricks of the trade from some of the best producers in the Big Apple. His personal life intervened and took him first to Melbourne, then Berlin and finally Budapest. Eddie stayed close to the music scene in all of these places and ended up producing music for a number of artists as well as stage productions.

Steve Woodford | Songwriter

The bulk of Steve's experience comes from writing music for short films, documentaries and commercials, but being an avid gamer himself, he has also recently started dabbling in composing music for video games. Trained at the Royal College of Music, London, Steve originally has foundations in classical music but thanks to a songwriting scholarship at Berklee College of Music, Boston, he also feels comfortable in more contemporary settings.
